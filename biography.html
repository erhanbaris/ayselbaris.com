Manifesto









Find in Abstract


The world we know is the world we know… Maybe not. Considering the vast scale of diversity of human beings, the variations that can exist by the endless series of possibilities ... Languages, cultures, experiences, colors, acceptance and rejection, abandonment, reasons for happiness, causes of unhappiness and the diversity and variability of all of them ... Every person is shaped by the lives he lives, and as a result of the life weave, which is impossible to repeat, the only and unique one forms itself. While each of us is so unique. How can one be good and bad? Is beauty and ugly the same for all of us? Has it ever been the same? The image and message of the world we are in was bombarded In the face of mania, we are pushed to be the same to remain happy or look happy. Nowadays, not every visible is as it seems, the trust in the image is over. It is over just like: as the much word is light and the less word is heavy! He became a puppet of money and ideologies, shading his purity and dignity. In this world called Global where we are living, those who torn so that everything is the same tried to make the art the same. As long as people are different, they will be free in art. The more diverse our souls are, the world we belong to and their perspectives, the common desired happiness, the infinite shapes ... Why should the worlds we seek be one. Abstract Art is the wanted freedom, enthusiasm and calmness. It is you… It is Abstract Art that nurtures the spirit, listens to you and makes you listen to it differently each time…




Biography





  • Aysel Asenova/Aysel Barış was born in 22.04.1990/Bulgaria
  • 2005 Moved to Turkey after studying in Bulgaria until 2005.
  • 2009 Graduated from Edirne Anatolian Fine Arts High School
  • 2014 Graduated from Trakya University, Faculty of Fine Arts, Department of Painting.
  • 2015 Married with Ruslan Ognyanov Asenov and after that get the surname Aysel Asenova.
  • 2016 Graduated from Pedagogical Formation certificate program of Uludağ University Faculty of Education in Bursa/Turkey.
  • 2016 For 6 months, interned in Bursa Zeki Müren High School of Fine Arts as an arts teacher.
  • 2017 Graphical design and web design course at Bilge Adam Academy.
  • 2018 Master's degree from the Institute of Social Sciences, Trakya University, Programme: Painting .
